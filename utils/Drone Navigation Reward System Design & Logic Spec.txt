================================================================================
                      PHASE 1-8: BASIC FLIGHT & LOCOMOTION
================================================================================
INTENT: Teach the drone to stay airborne, face the target, and fly straight 
without hitting walls in wide-open or simple grid environments.
Maximum Step Reward Cap: +5.0

A. Obstacle Avoidance (Distance-Based Safety Layer)
- Critical Front:   d < 1.0m       -> -12.0 penalty
- Danger Front:     1.0 <= d < 1.5 -> -4.0 * (2.5 - d)
- Floor Danger:     d < 1.2m       -> -8.0 * (1.2 - d)
- Floor Proximity:  d < 1.5m       -> -5.0 / (d + 0.05)
- Side Walls:
    - Critical:     d < 0.7m       -> -10.0 * (0.7 - d)
    - Danger:       d < 1.0m       -> -4.0  * (1.0 - d)
    - Warning:      d < 1.5m       -> -1.5  * (1.5 - d)
- Proximity Bubble: 2.5 < d < 6.0  -> -20.0 * exp(-0.8 * d) (Applied to all sides)

B. Goal Progress (Primary Signal)
- Formula: Clipped distance delta (-2m to +2m) multiplied by a dynamic scalar.
- Scaling: The closer the delta is to max progress (2m), the lower the scalar (ramps from 8.0 down to 2.0) to prevent velocity exploitation.

C. Direction & Velocity Alignment
- Target Alignment: +1.2 * (Velocity Vector dot Goal Vector).
- Lateral Penalty:  -0.2 * Lateral Speed * (1.0 - max(alignment, 0)). Punishes sliding sideways when not facing the goal.
- Vertical Penalty: -0.1 * Vertical Speed * (1.0 - abs(Goal Z)). Punishes climbing/diving if the goal doesn't require it.

D. Corridor Centering
- Trigger: Active when total corridor width (left + right) < 7.0m.
- Center Reward: Up to +4.0 based on perfect mathematical center.
- Off-Center Pen: -1.5 * (offset - 0.35) if offset ratio > 35%.
- Narrow Pen:     -0.5 * (6.0 - width) if corridor is tighter than 6m.

E. Orientation Stability
- Pitch: >8 degrees (-1.5 multiplier), >5 degrees (-0.1 multiplier).
- Roll:  <5 degrees (+0.2 stable bonus), <=8 degrees (-0.1 multiplier), >8 degrees (-1.5 multiplier).

F. Control Smoothness & Environment
- Action Mag:    -0.05 per step based on raw action size.
- Action Jitter: -0.10 based on difference from previous step.
- Wind Drift:    -1.5 * (Velocity dot Wind) if wind > 0.5 m/s and drone is being pushed by it.
- Time Pressure: -0.03 per step.

================================================================================
                    PHASE 9-12: ADVANCED URBAN NAVIGATION
================================================================================
INTENT: Teach the drone to navigate tight city blocks, perform sharp 90-degree
turns, brake for obstacles, and fly holonomically.
Maximum Step Reward Cap: +12.0

A. Phase 9 Curriculum Bridge (Episodes 1 - 200)
- Blends Phase 1-8 reward logic with Phase 9-12 logic.
- Starts at 70% old / 30% new at Episode 1, smoothly decaying to 0% old / 100% new by Episode 200.

B. Enhanced Safety & Evasion System
- Ceiling:      Z < -12.0m (Phase 9) or -7.2m (Others) -> -8.0 * absolute overrun.
- Floor:        Z < 1.2m -> -8.0 * (1.2 - d).
- Auto-Brake:   Front < 4.0m AND moving forward > 1.4 m/s -> -5.0 * velocity * (4.0 - d).
- Side Walls:   Strict tolerances. <1.0m (-15.0 multiplier), <2.0m (-8.0 multiplier), <2.5m (-2.0 multiplier).
- Front Evasion (Dynamic Obstacles):
    - When an obstacle is <7m ahead:
        - Climbing (>0.15 m/s): Up to +8.0 reward.
        - Sinking  (<-0.1 m/s): -4.0 penalty.
        - Doing neither:        -2.0 penalty.
- Front Standard: < 1.5m (-12.0 penalty), < 2.5m (-5.0 * (2.5 - d)).

C. Intersection Transit Control
- Entry Bonus:     +4.0 exactly once upon entering intersection boundaries.
- Speed Control:   If inside, moving 0.3 to 1.2 m/s grants +3.0/step.
- Speed Penalty:   If inside, moving > 1.6 m/s grants -2.0 * velocity.

D. Anti-Hovering (Locomotion Enforcement)
- Trigger: Velocity < 0.55 m/s while > 3.0m away from the target.
- Intersection: -0.4 penalty (allows mild slowing for turns).
- Open Road:    Dynamic penalty scaling from -0.5 up to -3.0 over the first 300 episodes of the phase.

E. Holonomic Locomotion & Alignment
- Alignment: Calculates dot product of velocity and goal vectors.
- Phase 12 Boost: If alignment > 0.85 AND front clear (>8m) AND far from goal (>15m) -> +3.0 * alignment * min(vel, 2.5).
- Standard Boost: +1.5 * alignment * min(vel, 2.0).
- Diagonal Pen:   Prevents "corner cutting" by penalizing simultaneous high X and Y velocity: -3.0 * (vx * vy) if product > 0.15.

F. Orientation & Smoothness (Strict Mode)
- Yaw Lock:      Strict penalty if yaw deviates > 0.1 radians (-5.0 * yaw).
- Pitch & Roll:  Same mathematical bounds as Phase 1-8.
- Action Mag:    -0.05 per step.
- Action Jitter: -0.15 per step (Stricter than Phase 1-8).

G. Lateral/Vertical Correction & Environment
- Lateral Slide: -0.2 * lateral speed * (1.0 - max(alignment, 0)).
- Vert Drift:    -0.1 * vertical speed * (1.0 - abs(Goal Z)) (Disabled during dynamic obstacle evasion).
- Wind Drift:    Same mathematical bounds as Phase 1-8.
- Time Pressure: -0.12 (Phase 12) or -0.07 (Phase 9-11).
- Rebate:        +0.04 returned in Phase 12 if cruising fast and accurately in open space.
